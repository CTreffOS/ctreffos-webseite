Chaostreff Osnabrück

The Chaostreff Osnabrück e.V. is a loose group of people with interest in the areas of security, cryptography, alternative operating systems, free software, network policy and many other topics. Interested people are always welcome at our regular meetings.

Projects

We have a number of projects on which we work more or less regularly. Basically, everything we do is voluntary and we do it because it is important to us. Do you have ideas for projects or would you like to contact us? Write us an email or just come to one of our meetings.

Chaos macht Schule

Chaos macht Schule is an initiative of several Erfa circles of the Chaos Computer Club (CCC), which have been working together with various educational institutions since 2007. The aim of the project is to strengthen pupils, parents and teachers in the areas of media competence and technical understanding.

codingchaOS

Together with the Student Council of Mathematics/Computer Science we organize the codingchaOS: A competition that aims to solve small programming tasks as quickly, briefly and elegantly as possible and to score as many points as possible.

Join us

We are a registered association and currently have about 15 members. In addition we are pleased about support, all the same whether by money, knowledge or perhaps even a space. We have been dreaming about our own hackspace for a long time and are looking for rooms that offer internet (as fast as possible), are as accessible as possible from the city centre and railway station, are affordable and lockable. Toilets and heating would be great, then we are quite happy. So: Let us know who knows what!
