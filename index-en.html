The Chaostreff Osnabrück is a group of people interested in topics like security, cryptography, alternative operating systems, free (libre) software, internet related politics and what else comes to mind.

We are meeting every fourth Monday of a month at 7pm in the Zauber von OS located in the city of Osnabrück. People interested in joining or visiting us are welcome to do so.
